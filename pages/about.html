About Me

My name is Becca, but my friends call me Little Gremlin! Honestly, I'm a bit awkward so most of my photo shoots are some degree of uncomfortable. I am based out of the land flowing with milk and honey: Provo, Utah!

My passions include being a shut-in, watching Gilmore Girls over and over again, spending time with my pet fish Kevin, and I guess photography too.

Honestly, I'm not very good at taking photos. Most of the time I move the camera before the photo has taken, but then it has a blurry effect which is kind of fun. So I just tell people that was intentional. My photographing interests include: parking lots, the contents of college students' fridges, and discarded receipts.
